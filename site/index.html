How it works

When a company joins the portfolio, we deploy a full-time W2 Support Partner hired through shuckerVC's LLC. The company pays their salary at cost—zero markup, zero dilution—and they embed with the leadership team through its highest-growth phase.

What they own

Everything that isn't product or engineering: recruiting and HR, monthly book closing and forecasting, growth and marketing, office ops, SOC 2, and Series A prep.

Why it aligns

Support Partners earn from our GP carry pool, not portfolio equity—so they're aligned with the fund's success, not the fate of any single company.
