**Card** — the standard content surface: white, soft warm shadow, 16px radius.

```jsx
<Card interactive>
  <h3>Time to Market</h3>
  <p>Bringing a product to market quickly is crucial…</p>
</Card>

<Card tone="ink">Dark feature card</Card>
```

Tones: `light` · `muted` · `ink`. `interactive` adds a hover lift. `padding`: `none` · `sm` · `md` · `lg`.
